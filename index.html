I am an Assistant Professor in the Information School at the University of Washington where I teach and do research in the areas of systems analysis and design, software sustainability, and data curation. I also currently serve as the Technical Director of the Qualitative Data Repository where I lead a small development team in building open-source tools to facilitate transaprent social science research. My Ph.D. is in Information Science from the University of Illinois.
